Swimming

Everyone wants to have a nice time with family on any vacation. We have some of the best swimming pools in Mumbai. Spend your day at our pools and take your experience to a new level with our water park for people of all age groups. Our services start at 9am in the morning and go on till 5pm in the evening. Separate pools for children are also available. We also rent out swim suits for adults as well as children, so don't worry if you forget to bring your swim suits with you. Come and have a relaxing day with us.

Children Playroom

The playroom provides a bunch of toys and rides for children of all age groups. The playroom opens at 9am in the morning and closes at 9pm at night. Come and play with your children and have a good time. Babysitters available to take care of children for the entire time.


Yoga Classes

What better way to start your day other than yoga? Come attain peace of mind and start your day with our daily yoga classes with trainers and other guests. We start daily at 6am and go on till 9am. Leave anytime. Come and start your day in the best possible way with your loved ones. Stay fit, stay healthy!
