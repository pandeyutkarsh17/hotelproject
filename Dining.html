Buffet

The Grand Palace brings to you a varied array of multi-cuisines inspired from the Mediterranean, European, Mexican and Indian regions. The extensive and comfortable seating arrangement, the seamless flow of aroma from one table to another will entice you to sit down for a warm lunch. The variety of grills and kebabs on the offer are enough to tempt the strictest of weight watchers to indulge in a pure orgy of flavours. The buffets have become popular motivations to take the family out for that long lazy Sunday lunch over good food and seasoned wine. If your teenage child throws food tantrums, you are safe to hand over the menu with a variety of Malaysian, Mexican, Japanese and American delicacies which would surely confuse and calm all demands and jarred nerves! We are happy to say that our efforts have been received with much love and in spite of being barely a few years old, The Grand Palace has won the Times Good Food Awards for Best Multi-Cuisine Buffet.
